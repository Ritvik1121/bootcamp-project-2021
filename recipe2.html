Strawberry Milk

Who hasn't heard of strawberry milk before? A perfect combination of sweet tasting strawberrie combined with milk to make a sweet tasting drink anyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 14 strawberries
  • 60g of sugar
  • 1/2 liter of milk

Cooking Intrucitons

  1. Wash the strawberries and crush 6 of them.
  2. Take the crushed strawberries into a bot and mix it with sugar. Heat the pot at medium heat
  3. Let the strawberries boil for 2-3 minutes before letting them cool.
  4. With the remaining strawberries, dice them and add to your bottle.
  5. Add the syrup created by the boiled strawberries and milk to the bottle and stir well to enjoy!.
