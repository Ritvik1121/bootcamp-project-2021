Spam Musubi

An authentic Hawaiin dish comprised of grilled spam layed atop a block of rice and warpped with nori.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of spam
  • Oyster sauce
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • Nori -> roasted seaweed used for sushi
  • Cooked sushi rice

Cooking Intrucitons

  1. Mix equal parts Oyster sauce and soy sauce into a boil along with some sugar.
  2. Slice desired amount of spam slices.
  3. Marinate spam in the micture just created.
  4. Fry the spam after taking it out of the marinade.
  5. Lay out a strip of nori and place the block of rice on top.
  6. Add the spam slice on top and then wrap the nori around the top and use a little water to seal it if necesarry.
  7. Enjoy!
